Mission Statement

We are Mission Box-UW Madison, a new student led organization here on campus! Our club was started with inspiration from the national organization Freedge, and our goal is to lower food insecurity in the Madison area. Our mission revolves around our strategically located Mission Box, a source of non-perishable food items and water that is freely available to all who need it. Additionally, we welcome members of the community to donate their own non-perishable items and/or water, to bridge the gap between the University and residential community of Madison. We also strive to eliminate the stigmatization of people experiencing food insecurity by allowing people to grab their own food rather than serving it to them.

Location of Our Mission Box

Our Mission Box can be found at 711 State St on the West side of the University Bookstore!

Our Promises to the Community

  • Our mission box will be stocked by University and residential members of the community, and we will establish connections with food sources and other clubs in the Madison area to ensure our box is always stocked.
  • Our mission box will have a folder attached to it that contains the Public Health of Madison and Dane County's Community Resources brochure.
  • We will have certain members check the mission box daily and remove expired items, restock resource brochures, etc.
  • We will have certain members deep clean the box weekly to ensure safety and cleanliness in the community.
  • We will have monthly fundraisers at the start of each month (if needed) to incur funds for stocking the mission box.
  • We will have monthly meetings with food provided at the end of each month in which people can sign up for which days of the following month they would like to perform box checks or cleans.
  • Members will be required to sign up for at least 4 box checks and 1 box clean per month to be deemed active members of the club.
  • We will meet and lobby Wisconsin legislators once per semester on bills that directly or indirectly cause food insecurity and food waste.
  • We will perform food drives for non-perishable items to stock our box with once per semester or more if needed.
  • We will conduct grocery store runs to purchase items for the box if/when deemed necessary.
